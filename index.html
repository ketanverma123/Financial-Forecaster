Financial Forecaster Tool for Companies

This tool predicts future financial metrics based on historical data.

Steps to Upload:

  • File format: Upload a CSV or Excel file (.csv, .xls, .xlsx).
  • Required columns: Ensure your file has the following columns in the exact order:
    • Year
    • Quarter
    • Company
    • Revenue
    • Gross Profit
    • Net Income
    • Earning Per Share
    • EBITDA
    • Share Holder Equity
    • Current Ratio
    • Debt/Equity Ratio
    • ROE
    • ROA
  • Important: Ensure the column names match exactly and the financial data is accurate for better predictions.
  • Once uploaded, predictions for future quarters will be generated, along with performance insights and suggestions.
Accepted formats: CSV, Excel (.xls, .xlsx)
